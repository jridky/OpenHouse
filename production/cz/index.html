About Open House 2020

To ensure the safety and well-being of the attendees, we have decided to rebuild Red Hat Czech Open House as a virtual event. Instead of a physical event in our Brno office, you can enjoy live competitions, chatting with Red Hatters, and live and pre-recorded sessions about Red Hat technology, culture, and teams.

Do you have any question? Please, contact us at openhouse@redhat.com.
