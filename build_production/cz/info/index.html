WorkAdventure FAQ

Hi and welcome to the Open House WorkAdventure map!

The platform is made to recreate the social experience of Open House in a game-like environment. You can walk around and explore Red Hat Brno office. You can meet people in the hallways and have a chat or join them by the tables and enjoy the snacks together.

Q: How does this work?

A: You can move around pressing keys: W/A/S/D (or arrow keys), you can explore the map and most importantly you can connect with others!

Q: How do I connect with others? Walk up to them!

A: Every player has a circle around them, when you come into the circle a call will start. The hallway calls are limited to 4 people.

Q: Is there a participant limit for the call?

A: Yes, in hallways, regular rooms and outside you can only talk to upto 4 people. Meeting rooms with names have a bigger capacity.

Q: How can I connect with Red Hatters on the map?

A: If you are not sure who is a Red Hatter, there is a red stall in the main entrance of the building, if you walk up to it a call with a Red Hatter will start! When the Jitsi starts, the page will split in half, WorkAdventure on the left side and the call on the right side.

Q: I want to view extra content on the map (info / photos), how can I do it?

A: Generally, you should walk close enough to the icons representing extra content (info / photos ). When the link loads, the page will split in half, WorkAdventure on the left side and the content on the right side.

Q: How can I manage settings?

A: In the bottom right corner you have a small camera and microphone, use that to manage your audio/video while being on the map. In the top left corner you have a mug and a mail, the mug can be used to edit your profile, character, name and more! More about the mail icon below.
